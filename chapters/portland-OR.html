Chapter Summary

Going into its third year, Hope For Homeless club is a student-run service club that works towards eliminating the stereotypes that come with homelessness and doing our part to end it in our community. Lincoln High Schoolâ€™s Hope For Homeless club is the main chapter of Kids First Project, a nonprofit organization that brings programs and services to homeless youth. Now a global chapter, Hope For Homeless clubs exist all around the world.

We have donated over one thousand dollars through bracelet fundraisers, clothing and food drives, and serving countless meals. Recently, we created a civics and government class at Portland Homeless Family Solutions to teach parents their civic rights.

We have been interviewed by KATU News, written about by the Cardinal Times, and recognized by PHFS through the Dr. Arnold Rustin Award and the City Club of Portland through the 2015 Civic Award.
