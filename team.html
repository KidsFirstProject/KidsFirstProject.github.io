Who we are

We’re a group of like minded individuals who are passionate about social justice and youth empowerment who have come together to bridge the gap between a child and the resources needed to reach their full potential and reverse the intergenerational cycle of poverty.

Board of Directors

Alisha Zhao
Executive Director

Alisha Zhao is currently a junior at Lincoln High School. With a deep passion for human rights and social justice, she is the first youth to ever serve on Portland's Human Rights Commission, represented Oregon as a 2015 ANNpower Fellow and 2015 NCAM Youth Ambassador, and aspires to work for the United Nations someday.
